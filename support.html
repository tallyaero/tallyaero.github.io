Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All flight data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers. Your logbook data stays on your phone or tablet.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings in the app and tap Backup. This saves your logbook to a file on your device. You can share this file via email, AirDrop, or cloud storage for safekeeping.

How do I import flights from another logbook app?

Export your flights as a CSV file from your current logbook app (ForeFlight, LogTen Pro, MyFlightbook, etc.), then use the Import feature in TallyAero to bring them in.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. TallyAero works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection.

How do I export my logbook?

Go to the Export section in the app. You can export your flights as CSV for use in spreadsheets or other logbook apps.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Your data is stored on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data will be deleted. Use the Backup feature before uninstalling to save your logbook.

Is there a desktop version?

TallyAero is currently available for iOS and Android. A desktop version is in development.
