Questions

How does the public intake URL work? ▼
You register a vanity URL like tallyaero.com/apply/your-name. Share it with any student — they fill out a structured checkride package with flight times, documents, endorsements, and aircraft info. Everything flows into your pipeline. No TallyAero account needed for the student.
Do I really get my own website? ▼
Yes. Every DPE with a TallyAero profile gets a Google-indexed professional page showing your bio, exam types, pricing, availability, and reviews. Students searching "DPE near me" find you directly. It auto-updates from your portal data — zero maintenance.
How does the applicant package work? ▼
TallyAero students get one-tap package generation: 8710 flight times, endorsements, medical, knowledge test, aircraft airworthiness docs — all pulled from their logbook automatically. Non-TallyAero students upload documents through the intake wizard with a CFR-based checklist for their specific certificate type.
What about students who call or email instead of using the portal? ▼
Quick-add them in two taps — name and certificate type. They're in your pipeline immediately. You can send them your intake URL to fill in details later, or manage them entirely within the portal.
Does the calendar sync with Google Calendar? ▼
Yes. Your DPE calendar generates a subscribe URL that works with Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook. Checkrides, signoffs, flight reviews, and personal blocks all sync automatically.
Can schools request checkrides on behalf of students? ▼
Yes. Schools with linked DPE relationships can submit individual or batch requests. You see school-submitted, individual, and public intake requests in the same pipeline.
Is this really free? ▼
Yes. The DPE portal is completely free for all Designated Pilot Examiners. No tier, no subscription, no feature limits. We make money from flight school subscriptions, not DPE fees. The more DPEs connected, the fewer checkrides get cancelled.
