What No Other DPE Tool Has

There is no DPE-specific software in aviation. We're building the first one — and it's free.

Unified Checkride Pipeline

Every checkride request — Part 61, Part 141, accelerated — in one view. Filter by status, sort by readiness score, see who's actually ready. No more juggling emails, texts, and phone calls from three different schools.

Pre-Checkride Validation & Readiness Scoring

Full eligibility check against FARs before the student can even apply. Hours, endorsements, documents, written test — all validated automatically. A 0-100 readiness score computed from real logbook data tells you at a glance who's ready, who's close, and who needs more time. Stop wasting trips.

Cryptographically Verified Applicant Records

Every flight backing a student's hours has a trust envelope — cryptographically hashed, co-signed by their CFI, with a complete amendment ledger. When you review an applicant package, you're not trusting handwritten totals. You're looking at provable, auditable records.

Auto-Generated Applicant Packages

Packages built from the student's actual logbook: 8710 data, endorsements, training history, CFI recommendation, document wallet, deficiency history. Everything you need, nothing missing, nothing manually totaled. If something's wrong, the system catches it before you do.

Bi-Directional School Linking

Connect with flight schools directly. You offer, or they invite. Configure which milestones trigger your notifications per school. See student progression early — get notified when students hit 80% ready, fully eligible, or package complete. Know who's coming before they call.

Batch Scheduling

Schools with 8 PPL candidates ready in March? See it on your batch view. Daily automated detection of batch-ready groups. Schedule efficiently instead of one-off requests. Schools can submit individual or batch requests through their linked relationship.

Questions

Do I need a TallyAero account to receive checkride requests? ▼
No. Students can send outreach emails to any DPE. You get a secure link to respond without creating an account. If you want the full portal experience — pipeline, batch scheduling, examining log — create a free account.
How does the applicant package work? ▼
When a student requests a checkride, TallyAero auto-generates a package from their logbook: 8710 data, endorsements, training history, documents, CFI recommendation, and deficiency history. You review it before the checkride — no surprises.
What if a student isn't ready? ▼
The eligibility check validates all FAR requirements before a student can apply. If they apply before being ready, you see exactly what's missing in the package. Readiness score tells you at a glance.
Can schools request checkrides on behalf of students? ▼
Yes. Schools with linked DPE relationships can submit individual or batch requests. You see school-submitted and individual requests in the same pipeline.
Is this really free? ▼
Yes. The DPE portal is completely free for all Designated Pilot Examiners. No tier, no subscription, no catch. We want every examiner on the platform because connected DPEs mean fewer cancelled checkrides for everyone.
