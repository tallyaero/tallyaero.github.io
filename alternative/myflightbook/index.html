What TallyAero does differently

Modern UI without the price tag. MyFlightbook proved free works. TallyAero's free tier adds what MyFlightbook can't — a modern interface, offline-first architecture, ADS-B auto-logging, and AI regulatory tools.

Offline-first, not internet-required. MyFlightbook needs connectivity. TallyAero stores 60+ tables locally on your device. Log flights at a grass strip with no signal — it syncs when you're back online.
