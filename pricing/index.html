Questions

Is the logbook really free for students? ▼
Yes. The Free tier covers everything a student pilot needs from first flight through PPL — no time limit, no trial expiration.
What does "one-time purchase" mean? ▼
Basic, Professional, and CFI logbook tiers are a single purchase. Pay once, own it forever. Only add-ons like DashTwo AI, OCR import, and military conversion are separate purchases or subscriptions.
Can I link my logbook to a school? ▼
Yes. Any logbook tier can connect to a school running TallyAero School Management. Your personal logbook stays yours — the school sees what you authorize.
How does school pricing work? ▼
Tiered by operation size — aircraft count, student seats, and features needed. Built for schools with 2 planes or 200. No modular hub stacking.
Is DashTwo AI included with the school plan? ▼
DashTwo AI for school operations is available as an add-on to any school tier. Individual pilots can add DashTwo to any logbook tier separately.
Is the DPE portal really free? ▼
Yes. Every DPE gets full access to the DPE portal at no cost — no tier, no subscription. We want every examiner on the platform.
When will pricing be finalized? ▼
We're finalizing pricing with our design partners now. Exact prices will be published on this page — no sales call required.
Will there be contracts? ▼
No long-term contracts. We're building something good enough that you'll want to stay.
