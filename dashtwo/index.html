"Am I current to take passengers on a night VFR flight this Friday?"
Generic AI

To carry passengers at night, you need at least 3 takeoffs and 3 landings to a full stop within the preceding 90 days during the period beginning 1 hour after sunset to 1 hour before sunrise. You also need a current flight review and medical certificate. Check your logbook to see if you meet these requirements.

✗Cannot check YOUR logbook
✗Generic requirements only
✗Makes you do the work yourself
DashTwo AI3 sources

Based on your logbook: You have 21 landings in the last 90 days, but only 1 night landing (KSDL, Mar 12). Per §61.57(b), you need 3 night landings to a full stop within the preceding 90 days. You are NOT current for night passengers. Your medical (Class 3, exp Aug 2026) and flight review (completed Jan 2026) are both current. You need 2 more night full-stop landings before Friday.
