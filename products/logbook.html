What Makes This Different

Four core capabilities that set TALLYAERO Logbook apart—plus full configurability for any aircrew position.

1. Military Conversion Engine

Transitioning from military to civilian? You have thousands of hours in military format that don't map cleanly to FAA categories. TALLYAERO understands both worlds.

What It Handles:
  • All military flight record formats translated to FAA categories
  • Instructor Pilot time → CFI equivalency mapping
  • NVG time, formation time, tactical time—all tracked
  • Multi-crew to single-pilot conversions
  • Airline format exports for every major carrier

2. Multiple Import Avenues

Get your existing flight time into TALLYAERO however works best for you. No single "right way."

Your Options:
  • Manual Entry — Efficiency-optimized layout with hotkeys. Fingers never leave the keyboard. Tab-through entry faster than any other logbook.
  • CSV/JSON/Excel Import — Map your columns to TALLYAERO fields, or create custom fields. You control the mapping.
  • AI-Powered OCR — Scan your logbook pages. AI interprets entries into a reviewable baseline. You audit and approve everything before it goes in.
  • Contextual Entry — Just want your totals? Create a single contextual entry that captures previous totals and feeds the master. Start fresh from here.

3. Context Logbooks

Flying multiple types? Instructing at one school, building time at another? Running concurrent logbooks? TALLYAERO handles all of it.

How It Works:
  • Create separate context logbooks for each avenue of flying
  • View and manipulate each logbook individually
  • Master Logbook compiles and totals everything automatically
  • See individual contexts or the complete picture—your choice
  • Each context can have its own layout, fields, and preferences

4. Training Integration

All CFR requirements and appendix requirements are attached to your profile. Track your real-time advancement toward your next certificate.

What You Get:
  • Part 61 and Part 141 requirements tracking
  • Proficiency tracker with "what to train" suggestions
  • Endorsements management and tracking
  • Milestones and certificate progress visualization
  • Simulator time analytics—what counts where
  • Minimum ground training requirements highlighted
  • Currency restoration flight plans to take to your CFI

Military to Civilian Flight Time Conversion

Transitioning from military aviation to civilian flying? Your thousands of hours in military format don't map cleanly to FAA categories. TallyAero's Military Conversion Engine understands both worlds and handles the translation automatically.

What We Convert

  • Navy/Marine Corps — NATOPS T/M/S codes translated to FAA categories
  • Air Force — ARMS and SHARP format entries fully supported
  • Army — IATF and flight record formats converted
  • Coast Guard — Flight hour documentation translated
  • Instructor Pilot (IP) time mapped to CFI equivalency
  • NVG flight time tracked separately with FAA-compatible notation
  • Formation time and tactical flight hours preserved
  • Multi-crew to single-pilot conversions with proper PIC/SIC splits

R-ATP Eligibility Calculator

Planning your airline transition? TallyAero calculates your Restricted ATP eligibility based on military flight time credits. See exactly where you stand on the 750-hour or 1,000-hour reduced minimums for military aviators.

Airline-Ready Exports

Generate reports formatted for all major carriers: United, Delta, American, Southwest, and regional airlines. Your military time is presented in the exact format hiring teams expect, with turbine PIC, total turbine, and multi-engine time broken out correctly.

Paper Logbook Digitization

Got years of flight time trapped in paper logbooks? TallyAero offers multiple pathways to bring your historical data into the digital age—without losing a single hour.

AI-Powered OCR Scanning

  • Scan your logbook pages for best OCR accuracy
  • AI interprets handwritten and printed entries
  • Entries converted into a reviewable baseline
  • You audit and approve every entry before it goes live
  • Works with damaged, faded, or difficult-to-read logbooks

CSV, Excel & JSON Import

Already have your logbook in a spreadsheet? Import directly from CSV, Excel, or JSON files. Map your columns to TallyAero fields, or create custom fields for unique data. Full control over the mapping process.

Photo Backup System

Don't want to digitize everything right now? Take photos of each page and attach them to a contextual entry. Your paper logbook is backed up in the cloud, searchable, and safe from fire, flood, or loss.

Damaged Logbook Recovery

Water damage? Coffee spill? Fire damage? Our OCR system can often extract data from damaged pages that would otherwise be lost. Combined with 8710 data and other records, we help reconstruct what was lost.

Pilot Currency & Proficiency Tracking

Never wonder if you're legal to fly again. TallyAero tracks every currency requirement and alerts you before anything expires.

What We Track

  • Flight Review (BFR) — 24-month expiration countdown
  • Instrument Proficiency Check (IPC) — Due dates and grace periods
  • Medical Certificate — Class I, II, III with BasicMed support
  • 90-Day Passenger Currency — Day and night tracked separately
  • Night Currency — Specific night takeoff/landing requirements
  • IFR Currency — Approaches, holds, and intercepting/tracking per 61.57
  • Type-specific currency — Complex, high-performance, tailwheel

Proactive Alerts

Receive SMS and email notifications with configurable lead time. Get reminded 30 days out, 14 days, 7 days—whatever works for your schedule. Never get surprised by an expired currency.

Currency Restoration Plans

Lapsed on IFR currency? TallyAero generates a currency restoration flight plan showing exactly what you need: how many approaches, holds, and tracking tasks. Hand it to your CFII and get current efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the TALLYAERO Digital Pilot Logbook.

How do I convert my military flight time to civilian FAA format?

TallyAero's Military Conversion Engine automatically translates military flight records from all branches—Navy/Marine Corps NATOPS, Air Force ARMS/SHARP, Army IATF, and Coast Guard systems—to FAA categories. It handles Instructor Pilot time to CFI equivalency mapping, NVG time, formation time, tactical time, and multi-crew to single-pilot conversions. The system generates airline-format exports accepted by all major carriers.

Can I digitize my paper pilot logbook?

Yes. TallyAero offers multiple import options including AI-powered OCR. Simply scan your logbook pages, and AI interprets entries into a reviewable baseline. You audit and approve everything before it goes into your digital logbook. You can also import via CSV, Excel, JSON, or manual entry.

Does TallyAero track pilot currency requirements?

Yes. TallyAero tracks day/night/IFR currency per 14 CFR 61.57, flight review (BFR) expiration, instrument proficiency check (IPC) due dates, medical certificate expiration, and all recency requirements. You receive SMS and email notifications with configurable lead time before currencies expire.

Can flight instructors (CFIs) use TallyAero for endorsements?

Yes. TallyAero includes comprehensive CFI tools with digital endorsement templates based on AC 61-65, student progress tracking, milestone alerts, CFI renewal tracking, and the ability to send endorsements electronically to students. CFI logs once and both instructor and student logbooks update.

Does TallyAero work offline?

Yes. TallyAero works offline with full functionality. Your data syncs automatically when you regain connectivity. You can also choose local-only storage if you prefer not to use cloud sync—your data, your choice.

Can I export my logbook for airline applications?

Yes. TallyAero generates airline-format reports for all major carriers, PRIA-compliant records, ATP flight time documentation, and 8710 form auto-population. The system provides accurate breakdowns of turbine PIC, multi-engine, cross country, night, and instrument time as required for airline applications.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

Your data is always yours. TallyAero offers full data export at any time in standard formats (CSV, JSON, PDF). There are no subscription hostage situations—you can download your complete flight history whenever you want, and local storage options ensure you always have access to your records.

Is TallyAero better than ForeFlight logbook or LogTen Pro?

TallyAero offers capabilities not found in other logbooks: military flight time conversion, AI-powered paper logbook OCR, context logbooks for multiple flying roles, and integrated training requirements tracking. Unlike cloud-only solutions, TallyAero offers local storage options so you're never locked out of your own data.
