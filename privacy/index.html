Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TallyAero ("we", "us") takes your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data We Collect

Account information (name, email), flight logbook data, school management data, and usage analytics. We collect only what's needed to provide the service.

How We Use Your Data

To provide TallyAero services, improve the product, and communicate with you about your account. We do not sell your personal data.

Data Storage

Data is stored on Google Cloud (Firebase/Firestore) with encryption at rest and in transit. Offline data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB.

Your Rights

You can export all your data at any time. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how the website is used. You can opt out using browser extensions or privacy settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@tallyaero.com.
