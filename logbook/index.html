What No Other Logbook Has

These are not incremental improvements. These are capabilities that don't exist anywhere else in aviation software.

Tamper-Proof Trust Chain with Blockchain Anchoring

Every flight is hashed, every edit is chained to the last, and batches are anchored to the Bitcoin blockchain — when you submit hours to an airline or DPE, every flight is auditable and provable.

CFI & DPE Co-Signatures

Instructor and examiner signatures are cryptographically bound to the exact record they signed — a DPE can verify your endorsement is unaltered, and material edits require re-approval.

Military-Native Logging & Conversion

30+ military fields, carrier ops, NVG, air refueling, WSO/NFO/loadmaster support, and automated military-to-civilian conversion per FAR 61.73 — the only logbook that translates your military career.

DashTwo AI Knowledge Engine

500K+ FAA documents, 40+ tools that read your logbook — not a chatbot, a reasoning agent that generates endorsements, preps checkrides, and cites exact FARs with a 6-layer anti-hallucination pipeline.

Endorsement Lifecycle with Prerequisite Enforcement

50+ FAA templates with prerequisite chains — can't issue solo XC without solo knowledge first, CFI record auto-built, remote signing from email, and every endorsement locked and preserved.

Proficiency Tracking with Skill Decay

50+ maneuvers tracked with individual decay rates — the system knows when your steep turn proficiency is degrading, not just that you logged one six months ago.
